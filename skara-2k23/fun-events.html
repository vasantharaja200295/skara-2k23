Found the match

In this found the match we keep 10 rows and 3 columns in the paper. We paste it in the floor . In that paper finder trace and leg trace will be printed .

Example : In first row 2 leg trace and 1 finder trace will available , in the second row 2 hand trace and 1 leg will available and other will similar like this . You have to paste your leg trace and hand in the create match of the paper or trace if you find the match in the given time you are the winner.

Rules : only you have to use the create hand trace ie ., Left means left , right means right.

Rules :
1) you cannot remove the headset
2) And never say the sentence by voice

Musical bumps

We’ll Play the music and have the players walk around the perimeter of the chair circle. When the music stops, everyone must immediately sit in a chair. The one person left standing it out. Remove another chair and continue until only one person is left.

Rules: Chairs must not be pushed, pulled, or moved in any way. Players must simply sit on the chairs without trying to “get” the chair in any other way that may cause injury to another player.
